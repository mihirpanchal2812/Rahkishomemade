About Us


Welcome to Rakhi's Homemade, where we've been making yummy treats for more than 15 years! Our goal is to make your celebrations extra special with our homemade cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and brownies. What makes us different is that we use really good ingredients that come from local sources. This way, our treats not only taste amazing but are also made with top-notch stuff. Check out our menu! We have all sorts of flavors in our cakes, from classic ones to special ones. And if you're into small, cute cupcakes or bite-sized cookies and brownies, we've got those too! But here's the cool part we also make treats that are good for you! Whether you're a vegan, gluten-free, or just like to eat healthy, we've got something for everyone.
